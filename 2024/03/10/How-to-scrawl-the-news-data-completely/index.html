How to scrawl the news data completely

2024-03-10 21:08

Recently, I was scrawling the news data. The target is simply web pages. So I use requests to get htmls and images.
Firstly, I downloaded the contens list of the news and try to extract urls of the adjacent pages.

I tried using pywebcopy to download, but I don’t know why the early urls could download nothing and I didn’t search out any answer.

Then I just used re and find to extract. However, I found maybe due to the orgnization of the content, I couldn’t download all the target news by scrawling.

Since the format of url is “www.domain.com/content/YearMMdd/{6:six-decimal numbers of total number of contents}”, and one html does not exceed 30kb, I decided to iterate the number and timestamp to make sure scrapying completely.

Today I found python has a limit of recurrsion, which needed to be reset bigger with
import sys
sys.setrecursionlimit(10000)

And now the connect breaks every hundreds of requests. I need to learn more skills of VPS and scrawling.
