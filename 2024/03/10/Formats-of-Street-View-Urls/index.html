Formats of Street View Urls

2024-03-10 22:47

While chekcing requests of street view api, I found the formats of the urls are typically like:
“https://streetviewserver.map/thumb?streetview-id= &positionparam1= &positionparam2= &key= &info”
which only has a thumb of very low resolution, or like

 "https://streetviewserver.map/tile?streetview-id= &tileaxis1= &tileaxis2= &key= & info"
which with enough resolution,

I wonder whether I could find higer resolution image by changing the query parameter, I have tried “img”, “full” with 404 results.
