https://serverfault.com/questions/4906/using-dd-for-disk-cloning
With some attentions:
1.”clone disks having bad blocks: dd if=/dev/sda of=/dev/sdb bs=4096 conv=sync,noerror –
Sam Watkins Nov 14, 2014 at 6:52”

2.”dd is most certainly the best cloning tool, it will create a 100% replica simply by using the following command. I’ve never once had any problems with it.
dd if=/dev/sda of=/dev/sdb bs=32M status=progress
